What is InstaDispatch?

InstaDispatch is a cloud-based delivery management software designed for courier companies, logistics providers, e-commerce businesses, and enterprises that manage last-mile delivery operations. The platform brings together automated dispatch, route optimization, real-time driver tracking, electronic proof of delivery (ePOD), customer notifications, and advanced reporting — helping you reduce delivery costs, improve efficiency, and delight your customers.

Key Features Of Delivery Management System

InstaDispatch delivery management software is loaded with advanced features and offers a range of flexible solutions to suit your delivery and courier business. Take a look, we think you'll agree.

🛣️

Route Optimization

Effective delivery route planning software helps businesses plan the most efficient route for delivery, reduce fuel costs, improve resource utilization, minimize delays, enhance customer experience, and make every delivery profitable.

📲

Digital Proof Of Delivery

Capture digital signature, OTP, barcodes, photos, comment, timestamp and geo-coordinates at delivery location and access them instantly in the dispatcher dashboard or via API.

📡

Real-Time Delivery Tracking & Alerts

Track your drivers in real-time on an interactive map. Keep your customers updated throughout the delivery cycle by sending them real-time SMS alerts at various stages.

⚙️

Automate Delivery Dispatch

Reduce your workload by automating your deliveries to your nearest delivery agent as per their availability. Drivers get a delivery alert on Driver's app as soon as they receive a new delivery task.

🏬

Warehouse Management

Manage warehouse operations hassle-free. Our system allows you to keep records of Intrahub Transfers, out for delivery, return orders and much more in just a few clicks.

🗺️

Delivery Zones

Define delivery zones and set their pricing for each city to provide courier services as per your business requirements.

🏷️

Branded & Customizable

Customize the solution with your company logo and domain name in order to build your own brand. Maintenance will be done by InstaDispatch.

💵

Cash On Delivery

Complete Cash on Delivery workflow starting from manual or ecommerce booking to cash collection by driver and report generation to deposit cash to the sellers.

📈

Reporting & Analytics

Make data-driven decisions using detailed reports on your performance. You can get complete details of the data and make the required analysis.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about InstaDispatch.

What is InstaDispatch? +

InstaDispatch is a cloud-based delivery management software that helps courier companies, e-commerce businesses, and logistics providers automate dispatch, optimize routes, track drivers in real-time, and manage proof of delivery — all from one platform.

How do I get an account? +

Simply book a demo or contact our sales team. We'll guide you through setup, onboarding, and configuration based on your business needs.

Is there any specific telecom carrier supported by Instadispatch? +

InstaDispatch supports a wide range of global SMS and telecom providers. You can also configure your own gateway as per your region.

Does Instadispatch Mobile App work offline? +

Yes, our driver app supports offline mode. Deliveries and POD data are stored locally and synced automatically once internet connectivity is restored.

Which platform and version does the Instadispatch Driver App support? +

The InstaDispatch Driver App is available on both Android and iOS, supporting all modern OS versions.

I have a unique method of charging for orders to my customers. Will Instadispatch support the way I structure my pricing? +

Yes, InstaDispatch offers fully customizable pricing rules — including by zone, weight, distance, service type, and account-specific rates.

Does InstaDispatch offer a free demo? +

Yes, you can book a free personalized demo with our team to see how InstaDispatch fits your delivery operations.

What integrations does InstaDispatch support? +

InstaDispatch integrates with leading carriers (FedEx, DPD, DSV, Parcelforce, Palletways), e-commerce platforms (Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento), and accounting tools — plus open APIs for custom integration.
