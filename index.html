Capture digital signatures, OTPs, barcodes, photos, timestamps, and geo-coordinates at the delivery point. Access them instantly in the dispatcher dashboard or via API.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about InstaDispatch.

What is InstaDispatch? +

InstaDispatch is a cloud-based delivery management software that helps courier companies, e-commerce businesses, and logistics providers automate dispatch, optimize routes, track drivers in real-time, and manage proof of delivery — all from one platform.

How do I get an account? +

Simply book a demo or contact our sales team. We'll guide you through setup, onboarding, and full configuration based on your unique business needs.

Does the InstaDispatch Mobile App work offline? +

Yes, our driver app fully supports offline mode. All deliveries and POD data are stored locally on the device and automatically synced once internet connectivity is restored.

Which platforms does the Driver App support? +

The InstaDispatch Driver App is available on both Android and iOS, supporting all modern OS versions on smartphones and tablets.

Can InstaDispatch support my custom pricing structure? +

Yes, InstaDispatch offers fully customizable pricing rules — by zone, weight, distance, service type, and account-specific rates. Whatever your pricing model, we support it.

What integrations does InstaDispatch support? +

InstaDispatch integrates with major carriers (FedEx, DPD, DSV, Parcelforce, Palletways), e-commerce platforms (Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento), accounting tools, and offers open APIs for custom integration.

Does InstaDispatch offer a free demo? +

Yes! Book a free personalized demo with our team to see exactly how InstaDispatch fits and optimizes your specific delivery operations.
